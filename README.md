The Prism Mod aims to overhaul Terraria and add many new bosses, npcs, items, biomes, and more.

[Join our discord!](https://discord.gg/wHCmEDU)

### So far we have added:

* 6 Weapons
* 5 NPCs
* 4 Consumables
* 4 Accessories (1 is Secret)
* 3 Materials
* 2 Armor sets
* 1 Mini-boss
* 1 Pet
* 1 Tile
